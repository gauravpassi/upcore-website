"50% of the leads our DSAs chase do not qualify. We find out after 5 days of effort."

The Lead Qualification Agent screens every inquiry before it reaches a DSA, checking income, employment, vehicle type, and existing EMI load, and routes only qualified prospects for follow-up.

Use Case 02
12-Day Disbursement Cycle
"An average vehicle loan takes 12 days to disburse. 9 of those are document chasing."
The Cost
Slow disbursals lose you deals to faster NBFCs. Borrowers do not wait.
What We Build For You
The Document Collection Agent chases every required document via WhatsApp, validates readability and name-matching, nudges for re-submission if quality fails, and packages the complete file for underwriting.
✓
Document collection drops from 9 days to under 48 hours
✓
60% fewer incomplete applications reaching underwriting
✓
Disbursement TAT cut in half with the same underwriting team

Agent Specialities
Your AI Workforce for Vehicle Loans & NBFCs
Agent 01

Lead Qualification

Screens every inquiry against your credit policy, verifies income and employment, checks vehicle type eligibility, and surfaces existing EMI load. ensuring DSAs only contact borrowers with a real chance of approval.

Agent 02

Document Collection

Chases required documents via WhatsApp, validates document quality and name-matching, flags errors immediately for re-submission, and creates organised file packages for underwriting. eliminating the longest TAT bottleneck.

Agent 03

Bureau Check Agent

Pulls credit bureau data automatically, surfaces negative flags and existing liabilities, calculates debt-to-income ratios, and produces a structured report for your underwriting team. removing manual CIBIL checks from your process.

Agent 04

Field Verification Coordinator

Manages field verification scheduling, tracks FV agent visits, logs property and employer verification details, and escalates inconsistencies to your underwriting team. ensuring accurate risk assessment before approval.

Agent 05

EMI Collections

Sends personalised payment reminders at optimal times, handles payment queries and UPI link distribution, tracks payment status across channels, and escalates only genuine defaults to your collections team. improving collection rates while reducing team load.
