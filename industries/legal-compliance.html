Agent Specialities
Your AI Workforce for Legal & Compliance
Agent 01

Contract Review

Reads contracts, flags non-standard clauses and deviations from your playbook, extracts key terms, and produces structured summaries, with human review required before any action, speeding first-pass reviews from hours to minutes.

Agent 02

Due Diligence

Processes data room documents at scale, categorises by type and risk, summarises findings, and flags material issues with supporting evidence, so deal teams review structured analysis, not document dumps.

Agent 03

Regulatory Monitor

Tracks regulatory circulars from RBI, SEBI, MCA, and sector bodies daily, maps new requirements to internal policies, and alerts relevant teams with action items and deadlines. ensuring zero missed compliance windows.

Agent 04

Legal Research

Researches case law, statutes, and regulatory guidance on specific topics, summarises findings with citations, surfaces relevant precedents, and produces structured research memos. accelerating legal analysis and opinion development.

Agent 05

Document Drafting

Drafts documents based on templates and instructions, incorporates required clauses and policies, flags deviations from standards, and produces structured drafts for lawyer review and editing. speeding document creation without sacrificing quality.
